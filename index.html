How It Works

Three steps from your artwork to a print-ready file.

Upload Your Art

Drop any image — JPG, PNG, WEBP, or paste from a URL. The tool auto-fits it to your chosen mat size with full bleed.

Customize

Add game zone overlays, enhance colors, upscale with AI, adjust zoom and position — or keep it simple with Quick Upload.

Download & Print

Export a 300 DPI JPG with proper DPI metadata — exactly what print-on-demand vendors need to produce a sharp, professional mat.

Quick Upload

Drop your art and get a print-ready 300 DPI JPG in seconds.

Advanced Editor

Full canvas editor with AI upscaling, overlays, recolor, and transform tools.

Batch Enhance

Auto color-correct multiple images and download them individually or as a ZIP.

Format Converter

Convert any image to JPG, PNG, or WEBP — single file or batch ZIP download.

Game Overlays

Apply pre-made zone layouts for MTG, Pokémon, and more at full print resolution.

300 DPI Output

Every export writes proper 300 DPI metadata — exactly what print vendors require.

About Playmat Studio

Playmat Studio is a free, browser-based image prep tool built for tabletop game players who want to print custom playmats with their own artwork. No installs, no subscriptions, no sign-up — just open the tool and start designing.

All image processing runs directly in your browser using the Canvas API and Fabric.js. Your artwork never leaves your machine, except for the optional AI upscaling and background-removal features, which use serverless Cloudflare Workers. Supports standard and extended mat sizes, plus specialty formats ranging from 10″×8″ up to 31″×12″.
