Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2025

Playmat Studio is a free, browser-based image preparation tool. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights under applicable privacy law including the GDPR (EU/UK) and the CCPA (California).

1. Data We Collect

We collect no personal information and require no account, registration, or login. The following data may be processed during normal use:

  • Image files you upload — processed locally in your browser. They are never transmitted to our servers except as described in Section 3.
  • Browser local storage — used solely to remember recent upload history within your own browser. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.
  • Contact form submissions — if you use the contact form, your name, email address, and message are transmitted to us so we can respond. This data is not shared with third parties and is retained only as long as necessary to address your enquiry.

2. In-Browser Processing

All core image-processing tasks — resizing, cropping, colour correction, format conversion, overlay rendering, and export — run entirely within your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API and the Fabric.js library. Your artwork is never uploaded to any server during these operations.

3. Optional Cloud AI Features

Two features optionally transmit image data to a serverless cloud environment:

  • AI Resolution Enhancer — sends your image to a serverless processing function to upscale it to higher resolution.
  • AI Frame Break (background removal) — sends your image to a serverless processing function to isolate the foreground subject.

These transmissions occur only when you explicitly initiate the feature by clicking the corresponding button and confirming the prompt. Image data sent to these functions is processed transiently and is not stored, logged, or retained after the response is returned to your browser. No metadata identifying you is attached to these requests.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, analytics scripts, or any third-party tracking technology. The site uses only browser local storage as described above.

5. Third-Party Services

All libraries used (Fabric.js and others) are loaded and executed locally. The site does not embed third-party widgets, social buttons, or advertising networks that could independently collect your data.

6. Data Retention

We hold no persistent user data on our servers. Local storage data (upload history) is retained in your own browser until you clear it via the “Clear History” button or your browser settings. Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to resolve your request.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & UK GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of any personal data we hold about you (limited to contact form submissions). To exercise these rights, contact us via the contact form on this site. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Your Rights (CCPA — California Residents)

We do not sell, share, or disclose personal information to third parties for commercial purposes. California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request deletion. Because we collect only contact form submissions and retain them for the limited purpose of responding to you, there is nothing to “sell” and deletion requests are honoured upon request.

9. Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information via the contact form, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in technology or law. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, use the contact form on this site.

How It Works

Three steps from your artwork to a print-ready file.

Upload Your Art

Drop any image—JPG, PNG, WEBP, or paste a URL. The tool auto-fits it to your chosen mat size with full bleed.

Customize

Add game zone overlays, enhance colors, upscale with AI, adjust zoom and position—or keep it simple.

Download & Print

Export a 300 DPI JPG with proper metadata—exactly what print-on-demand vendors need for a sharp, professional mat.

Features

Everything you need, from upload to print-ready file.

Editor

Full canvas editor with AI upscaling, zone overlays, recolor, transform tools, and one-click fit-to-mat. Drop an image or paste a URL and you’re ready to go.

Batch Enhance

Auto color-correct multiple images and download them individually or as a ZIP.

Format Converter

Convert any image to JPG, PNG, or WEBP—single file or batch ZIP download.

Game Overlays

Pre-made zone layouts for popular TCGs and CCGs, applied at full print resolution.

300 DPI Output

Every export writes proper 300 DPI metadata—exactly what print vendors require.

About Playmat Studio

Playmat Studio is a free, browser-based image prep tool built for tabletop game players who want to print custom playmats with their own artwork. No installs, no subscriptions, no sign-up—just open the tool and start designing.

Playmat Studio is a community-driven project designed for anyone who wants to create custom playmats with their own artwork. Every feature is informed by real user feedback and requests. There are no ads, no fees, and no account required—just a free tool that improves as more people use and share it. If there’s something you’d like to see added, reach out—community input directly shapes what gets built next.
